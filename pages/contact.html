FAQ

Q: Can I use my own data?

Yes! Upload a CSV with at least 30 rows containing time and Avg.Price (Rs./Kg) columns. The system auto-fills missing features and generates live predictions.

Q: How often are forecasts updated?

Default forecasts are pre-computed from our AI models. When you upload your own data, predictions are generated live in your browser using our statistical engine.

Q: Is the system free to use?

Yes. The project is open-source. See the repository LICENSE for details.
